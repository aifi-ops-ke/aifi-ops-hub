Payroll

Calculate monthly pay including base rates, transport allowance, and incentive deductions

📌 Pay Structure: TL = KES 52,000/month fixed | QAs = KES 40,000/month fixed | Operators FT/PT = KES 1,150/day | Trainees = KES 500/day stipend (no transport, no incentives) | Transport = KES 3,000/month (KES 500/day deducted for unexcused absence) | Incentive pool = KES 7,000 (KES 2,000 per Global error, KES 1,000 per Internal error) | Night transport (23:00–01:00 UTC) = KES 300/shift | OT: TL = KES 500/hr, QAs = KES 400/hr | Cover pay (absent account): TL & QAs = KES 400 each
